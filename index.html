The challenge today

Planning in England and Wales
is being held back by process,
not by lack of information.

Fragmented Siloed Rarely spatial Inconsistent formats Hard to benchmark Insight buried
The challenge today

Significant time is spent navigating fragmented datasets and reconciling inconsistent formats.

What this costs us

As government and industry work towards major housing and infrastructure targets, this creates delays in moving from evidence to action.

Planning shouldn’t be a data-gathering exercise; it should be a creative, strategic one.
So we built a digital placemaking platform · launched May 2026
One overarching principle

Free up time so built environment practitioners can make faster, more evidence-led and more confident decisions that shape towns, cities and regions.
